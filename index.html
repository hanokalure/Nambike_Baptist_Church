Our Mission

At Nambike Baptist Church, we are committed to sharing the love of Christ and building a community of believers who worship, grow, and serve together. Our mission is to reach people with the Gospel and help them grow in their faith.

Our Vision

We envision a church where every person experiences God's love, grows in their relationship with Christ, and serves others with compassion and grace.
