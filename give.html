Giving to the church is not "paying for services rendered." It is not a "fee." Giving to the church is an act of worship as you help fund the mission God has given us. This allows us to share God's love with people in Bidar and around the world. If that is your mindset, you will experience the reality that it is "more blessed to give than to receive" (Acts 20:35).

Thank you for your faithful financial support of Nambike Baptist Church as we serve together to live out our mission.
